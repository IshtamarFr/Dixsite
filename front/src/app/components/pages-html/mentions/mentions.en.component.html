Who are we?

Ishtamar.fr belongs to the Ishtamar Computer Science and Education Association, which promotes learning computer science in an eco-responsible way.

Our goal is therefore to use the least powerful and least polluting machines possible and to optimize code and server resources.

In addition, we promote learning computer science through these services, by raising awareness among users and institutions about the ecological impact of the machines used, too often oversized.

The Ishtamar.fr server is based in France, and is subject to French and European laws and regulations.

GDPR

The RGPD, General Data Protection Regulation, is a set of texts aimed at protecting the rights of European citizens.

The ishtamar.fr site does not contain any cookies or trackers, so your visits are not tracked. However, some services are only accessible to registered users.

When you register, ishtamar.fr collects some personal data:

  • Your username
  • Your email address
  • Your IP address
  • The dates and times of registration and validation

During subsequent visits, ishtamar.fr also collects:

  • The dates, times and IP addresses of the different connections and uses
  • User contributions (messages, photos, comments, etc.)

In accordance with the regulations, you have a right of access and rectification to your personal data. You can exercise this right by contacting us.

This personal data is neither sold nor shared with third parties, with the exception of administrative or judicial requisitions in accordance with applicable laws.

This personal data is only collected for the purpose of ensuring the proper functioning of the service, and for internal statistical purposes.

Ishtamar.fr does not use cookies or trackers. Therefore, no GDPR banner is present on this site.
